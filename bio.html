Our Journey

Our story began in the most unexpected yet magical way—at a winter camp. We only talked for a few minutes, just enough to leave an impression that lingered. Time passed, but something about him stayed with me. Then, on my birthday, he messaged me—reserved, unsure, but sweet. And somehow, that was all it took. I liked him from that very moment. What started as a quiet spark slowly grew into something truly special. This winter, everything came full circle. He came to Romania, and those days together felt like a dream. We went ice skating, laughing and slipping hand-in-hand, explored beautiful places blanketed in snow, and shared warm, meaningful moments. He met my parents too—it felt like everything was falling into place. It’s crazy to think how a short conversation at camp turned into the most beautiful and meaningful relationship I've ever had. AJ became my everything and I would have never guessed it if you have told me about it a little over a year ago. But it is truly the best thing ever.
