My Goals and Dreams for us that I think we share

1. Travel the world together

I pray that we will be able to travel the world together, to visit as many places and to make as many memories together as possible. I pray that God would use us anywhere we would go and that we could serve the Lord even in our visits.

2. Build our dream home

No matter where we will live and no matter what kind of house we will have, it will always be my home as long as you are with me. I can't wait to build a whole future with you and have our own place to call home. A place where we can return to at the end of the day and enjoy it together.

3. Start a family

The only thing that would make our house feel even more like a home will be when we bring our children to this world. When we will have 3 beautiful children that look like you and have your smile and your kind soul and character. We will teach them all about Jesus and will have a beautiful, blessed family. I already pray for our future children, and I know that you do too. Having a family with you is like my ultimate goal.

4. Ministry for God

Last but not least, the most important goal I have for us is to serve the Lord together. I pray that God will use us for his glory in as many ways as possible and that He will provide for us in everything. I pray that He will look with favor on us and that we will be together in heaven with Him and with our whole family (both mine and yours). I pray that people will come to God through us and that all the glory will be given to Him.

Together, we are unstoppable. Our journey is just beginning, and every day brings us closer to fulfilling our goals. I am grateful for you.
